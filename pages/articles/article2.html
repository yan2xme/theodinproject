Embracing Complexity: Ms. Zamora's Gentle Insights on Mental Health


×

On January 5, 2024, Ms. Angel Tuleno Zamora guided students through the delicate terrain of mental health. A graduate from Mindanao State University GenSan, also once an MNHS SSG President, renowned for her mental health awareness advocacy, Ms. Zamora acknowledged the challenge, stating, "Mental Health is a hard topic to discuss. It's complex based on religious and scientific beliefs."

Undeterred by the complexity, she gracefully shared her reflections, encouraging attendees to approach the subject with open hearts. In a tranquil setting, she led an introspective activity, allowing students to express unspoken thoughts. Eyes closed, the room became a sanctuary for authentic reflections, transcending the complexities.
